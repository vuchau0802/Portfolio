About Me

I am a Machine Learning and AI Engineer with a strong engineering foundation and a Master’s degree in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence). My recent experience at TELUS Digital focuses on the end-to-end development and optimization of Large Language Models (LLMs). In this role, I manage the full development lifecycle, executing Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) and supervised fine-tuning (SFT) pipelines to improve model reasoning and safety. I also design human-in-the-loop (HITL) evaluation datasets and conduct rigorous security audits of AI-generated code to identify vulnerabilities, such as SQL injection.

Prior to my work with LLMs, I served as an AI Software Development Intern at SitusAMC, where I specialized in building scalable machine learning pipelines for the financial sector. I engineered features from extensive macroeconomic and borrower datasets to process over 890,000 loan records for risk prediction. During this time, I developed and benchmarked various classification and regression models, achieving 98% accuracy in loan amount prediction and default probability evaluation. My work also involved creating automated ETL pipelines and executive-level Tableau dashboards to support data-driven decision-making.

My foundation in data-driven optimization was established as an Advanced Process Engineering (APE) Engineer at Techtronic Industries (TTI). In this role, I led the optimization of high-volume manufacturing lines through multivariate statistical modeling and DOE, reducing defect rates and improving process yields. I also automated reporting by developing Python-based ETL pipelines and KPI dashboards using IoT sensor logs and quality metrics, ensuring reproducibility and operational efficiency.
